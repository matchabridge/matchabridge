Matcha Preparation Guide

How to serve matcha in your café
[Video/Image: Traditional Matcha Whisk Technique]

Traditional Matcha

The authentic ceremonial preparation. Perfect for premium café experiences and customers seeking the traditional ritual.

Recommended Grade: Ceremonial Grade A
Preparation Steps:
  1. Sift 1–2 tsp matcha powder into a bowl
  2. Add 2–3 oz hot water (160–180°F / 70–80°C)
  3. Whisk with a traditional bamboo whisk in an M shape
  4. Whisk faster to create a light foam
  5. Serve immediately in a ceramic bowl
[Video/Image: Matcha Latte Preparation]

Matcha Latte

Smooth, accessible, and perfect for daily café service. Available in hot and iced versions.

Recommended Grade: Café Grade B
Hot Matcha Latte:
  1. Whisk ¾ tsp matcha + 2 oz hot water
  2. Steam 8–10 oz milk to 150–160°F
  3. Pour matcha into cup, add steamed milk
  4. Top with 1 cm milk foam
Iced Matcha Latte:
  1. Whisk ¾ tsp matcha + 2 oz hot water
  2. Pour into serving cup over ice
  3. Add 8–10 oz cold milk
  4. Stir and serve
[Video/Image: Matcha Smoothie Blend]

Matcha Smoothie

Vibrant, nutrient-dense, and seasonal. Mix & match with fruit for variety.

Recommended Grade: Café Grade B
Base Recipe:
  • ½ tsp matcha powder
  • ½ cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 cup milk (dairy or plant-based)
  • ½ banana or 1 cup seasonal fruit
  • ½ cup ice
  • 1 tbsp honey or agave (optional)
Seasonal Variations:
  • Spring: Mango + coconut milk
  • Summer: Pineapple + lime
  • Fall: Apple + cinnamon
  • Winter: Blueberry + almond milk
[Video/Image: Matcha Dessert Showcase]

Matcha Dessert

Deep, bold matcha complements rich, creamy desserts. Leverage your premium grade for menu differentiation.

Recommended Grade: Premium Grade A or Bold Grade
Popular Pairings:
  • Matcha Cheesecake – Blend ½ tsp into filling
  • Matcha Panna Cotta – Whisk into cream base
  • Matcha Soft Serve – 1 tsp per quart of ice cream base
  • Matcha Shortbread – ¾ tsp per recipe
  • Matcha Tiramisu – Dust over layers + blend into mascarpone

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about ordering and quality
Q: How long does shipping typically take?
A: Lead times vary by farm (10–14 days typical). Once your order is ready, we ship via expedited courier. International shipping available on request.
Q: What's the minimum order quantity?
A: Minimum order quantities (MOQ) range from 2–5 kg depending on the farm. Final details are shared after approval.
Q: Can I return or exchange matcha if we're not satisfied?
A: Yes. If you're not satisfied with quality or consistency within 14 days of delivery, we'll arrange a return or replacement. Contact us for details.
Q: Do you offer exclusive blends or private-label matcha?
A: Yes, custom blends and private labels are available for larger partners. Discuss your specific needs during the inquiry process.
Q: How is matcha stored to maintain freshness?
A: Our partner farms use nitrogen flushing and cold storage. We recommend keeping matcha in a cool, dark place or refrigerated once opened. Unopened packages stay fresh for 12+ months.
Q: Can I see lot-level traceability for my order?
A: Absolutely. Every order includes a certificate of traceability with harvest date, processing details, and pack date. Learn more on our Traceability page.
