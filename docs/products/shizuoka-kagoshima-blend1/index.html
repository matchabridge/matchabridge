Our Matcha

Shizuoka × Kagoshima

A Japan-sourced blend designed for lattes, with strong color, a rounded body, and a clean finish.

Where it comes from

Two regions. One cup.

Shizuoka
Kagoshima

Shizuoka

Clean mountain air and well-drained slopes produce a smooth, balanced character. In this blend, Shizuoka brings a cleaner finish and keeps the overall cup from feeling too heavy.

Kagoshima

Known for giving the blend color, depth, and presence. In this blend, Kagoshima helps create a thicker body and vibrant appearance, especially important in milk-based drinks.

Together, they create a matcha that feels bold enough for lattes, but still clean and composed.
Why this blend

Built to hold up in milk

Most matcha lattes lose color or structure in milk. This blend was designed to hold up.

A slightly stronger roast brings out a gentle toasty warmth.
The color stays visible and stable in milk-based drinks.
It is built for consistency, so the cup stays stable across batches.
