Where it comes from

Shizuoka

One Region, One Harvest

At the foothills of Mt. Fuji, cool temperatures, significant day-night swings, and exceptionally pure mountain water create the conditions for something distinct. The river mist and softly diffused light slow the growth of the leaves, concentrating their umami and sweetness into a profile that is refined, balanced, and traceable to a single place.

Every cup comes from one harvest, one region - and that clarity shows in the flavor.
Why this matcha

Character from one place

Single-origin matcha is chosen for character - one place, one harvest, one story in every cup.

Grown in river mist and soft diffused light, this Yabukita-based matcha develops a balanced profile of umami, gentle bitterness, and natural aroma.
With shading cultivation, it delivers a clean, refined finish that holds up well in both hot and iced preparations.
First-harvest leaves bring natural sweetness and layered depth, while the powder keeps a vibrant, transparent green even after roasting.
