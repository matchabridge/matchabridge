Shipping Information

General shipping guidance for wholesale and partner orders coordinated through MatchaBridge.

1. Shipping Scope

We coordinate shipments based on partner farm location, destination country, and order volume. Coverage may vary by product category and local import requirements.

2. Processing Time

Typical processing starts after order confirmation and may vary by harvest cycle, stock status, and quality checks. Estimated lead times are provided during order coordination.

3. Delivery Time Estimates

Transit times depend on destination, carrier availability, customs procedures, and seasonal demand. Any estimated dates are non-guaranteed unless explicitly confirmed in writing.

4. Shipping Fees and Duties

Shipping charges, customs duties, taxes, and import fees are determined by destination regulations and shipment terms. Responsibility is allocated according to agreed commercial terms.

5. Risk and Transfer

Risk transfer and title conditions follow the applicable purchase agreement or quoted commercial terms for each order.

6. Damaged or Missing Items

If items arrive damaged or incomplete, notify us promptly with photos, shipment labels, and order details so we can coordinate investigation and resolution.

7. Returns and Cancellations

Because tea products are often perishable and batch-specific, returns or cancellations are handled case-by-case and may be limited after production or dispatch has started.

8. Contact for Shipping Questions

For active shipments or pre-order logistics planning, please contact us through the wholesale inquiry form.
