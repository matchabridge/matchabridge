How it works

Matcha Bridge partners directly with identified producers and maintains a simple chain-of-custody record. Each shipment can be linked to a lot with documented harvest and packaging details.

1) Farm & Harvest
Harvest window recorded (e.g., First flush Apr–May)
2) Processing
Steaming, drying, sorting steps aligned with each lot
3) Milling
Milling date recorded when available; temperature-sensitive handling
4) Packaging
Nitrogen-flushed packaging and cold storage to preserve aroma & color
5) Export & Delivery
Shipment documents linked to lot records
