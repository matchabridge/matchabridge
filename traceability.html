How MatchaBridge Traceability Works

From harvest to your cafe
01

Harvest Window Documented

Each harvest is tracked with exact dates, weather conditions, and farming practices. You receive documentation of the harvest window (e.g., "Apr 15–22, 2026") and hand-harvest percentage.

What You Get: Harvest certificate, cultivar info, shading days, yield data
02

Processing Timestamp

Immediately after harvest, leaves are steamed, dried, and sorted. The processing date and method (stone-milled, jet, etc.) are recorded with exact timestamps.

What You Get: Processing sheet with steaming time, milling method, output spec, quality grade
03

Storage & Packaging

Matcha is stored under nitrogen in cold conditions. When packaged for your order, the packing date, lot number, and best-by date are recorded.

What You Get: Packing certificate with lot number (e.g., "UJ-2026-1F-03"), seal date, best-by date, storage conditions
04

Delivery to You

Your order arrives with a complete Certificate of Traceability that includes QR code linking to full farm and batch details, producer video, and quality photos.

What You Get: QR code, producer story/video link, lab analysis (when available), farm map, tasting notes, recommended recipes

Share the Story with Your Customers

Every Certificate of Traceability includes a QR code that links to:

  • Producer Video: Meet the farmers behind your matcha and hear their story directly.
  • Farm Photos: See the tea fields, processing methods, and storage practices.
  • Lab Analysis: Chlorophyll content, amino acid profile, and quality testing results (when available).
  • Recipes & Pairings: Recommended drinks and dishes that highlight this matcha's flavor profile.
  • Sustainability Notes: Certifications, farming practices, and environmental impact.

Print it, QR-link it on your menu, or display it at your counter. Customers will love knowing exactly where their matcha comes from.

Traceability FAQs

Q: How can I verify the authenticity of the traceability records?
A: All records are linked to the farm's official certifications and backed by video documentation. Each QR code is unique and timestamped. You can directly contact the farm to verify any lot details.
Q: What if I need additional traceability documents for regulatory compliance?
A: Contact us with your specific requirements. We can provide COAs (Certificates of Analysis), food safety certifications, and other compliance documents as needed.
Q: Can I see traceability data for past orders?
A: Yes. We maintain a complete archive of all orders and their associated traceability records for at least 3 years. Access your order history anytime via your account.
Q: Do you support other traceability standards (e.g., blockchain, third-party audits)?
A: Currently, we use traditional documentation with digital verification via QR codes. We're exploring blockchain and third-party certification partnerships for future releases. Let us know if this is important to your business.
